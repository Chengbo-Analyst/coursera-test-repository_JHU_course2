Menu

Chicken

Yami's chicken menu was a testament to the bird's delicious versatility. From the classic, perfectly roasted chicken with its crispy skin and juicy meat, to the bold and spicy Sichuan chicken, where the heat danced on your tongue, each dish showcased unique flavors and textures. The creamy chicken curry, the fragrant lemongrass chicken, the savory chicken stir-fry – Yami's menu promised a delightful journey through a world of chicken-based culinary delights.

Beef

Yami's beef menu was a carnivore's dream. From the melt-in-your-mouth braised short ribs, bathed in a rich, savory sauce, to the sizzling, perfectly seared steak with its crisp edges and tender interior, each dish was a testament to the chef's skill and passion. The fragrant beef curry, the spicy beef stir-fry, the flavorful beef pho – Yami's menu offered a diverse range of beef dishes, each promising a satisfying and memorable culinary experience.

Sushi

Yami's sushi menu was a vibrant kaleidoscope of flavors and textures. From classic nigiri, where perfectly seasoned fish rested atop a bed of vinegared rice, to elaborate maki rolls bursting with fresh ingredients, each bite was an explosion of umami. The delicate sashimi, the colorful dragon rolls, the spicy tuna rolls – Yami's menu offered a wide array of sushi options, each carefully crafted to tantalize the palate and transport diners to a world of Japanese culinary artistry.
